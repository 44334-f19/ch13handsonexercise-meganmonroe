Reinforce your website's SEO.

SEO, or search engine optimization, is the quickest way to get your website noticed. Good SEO uses keywords, using alt text on your images, and optimizing your headings.

Linking

Verify that all hyperlinks are working. Each page on your website should be reachable by a text hyperlink and should be descriptive. Do not use phrases such as more info or click here. Should use keywords as appropriate. This is important because there is a direct correlation between quality/quantity of links to your site and how much search traffic your site receives.

Valid Code

Valid code is not required by search engines, but a well structured and valid code is more easily processed by search engine robots which could help your placement in the search engine.
